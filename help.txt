PolyRatings Enhancer - Privacy Policy
Last Updated: February 11, 2026
Data Collection:
PolyRatings Enhancer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal user data. The extension operates entirely locally in your browser.
What the Extension Does:

Fetches publicly available professor ratings from PolyRatings.dev
Displays these ratings on the Cal Poly schedule builder page
Caches rating data locally in your browser for performance

No Tracking:
We do not track your browsing, collect analytics, or share any information with third parties.
